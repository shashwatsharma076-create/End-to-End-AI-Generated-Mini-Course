### 🎯 HOW TO USE GAMMA.AI

1. Go to **gamma.app**
2. Sign up / Login
3. Click **"Create with AI"**
4. Select **"Presentation"**
5. Paste the **final script** below
6. Choose theme: **"Modern Dark"**
7. Generate → Export as PDF/PPTX
