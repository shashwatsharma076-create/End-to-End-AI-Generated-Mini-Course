# 📝 LESSON 1: What is a Prompt?
## A 2-Minute Script for Beginner

---

### 🎬 HOOK (15 seconds)

**[START SCRIPT]**

*"Hey there! Ever wondered why sometimes AI gives you amazing results, and other times it's just... completely wrong?"*

*"Well, 99% of the time, the difference comes down to ONE thing: how you ask."*

*"Today, in just 2 minutes, I'm going to teach you the most important skill in AI - and that's learning how to PROMPT."*

*"[Raise eyebrows with curiosity]"*

*"By the end of this lesson, you'll know exactly what a prompt is, and why it matters so much."*

*"[Pause for emphasis]"*

*"Let's go!"*

---

### 📚 DEFINITION (30 seconds)

**[SCRIPT]**

*"So... what exactly IS a prompt?"*

*"A prompt is simply the instructions you give to an AI. It's your way of talking to the AI and telling it what you want."*

*"Think of it like this - you're the boss, and the AI is your super-smart employee who needs clear instructions to get the job done right."*

*"The better your instructions, the better the results. It's that simple!"*

---

### 🎓 ANALOGY (45 seconds)

**[SCRIPT]**

*"Let me give you a real-world example."*

*"Imagine you're a teacher and you need to tell your students to write an essay."*

*"Now, if you just say: 'Write something about history' - what do you think happens?"*

*"[Mimic confused student]"*

*"Your students will be confused! What's the topic? How long? What format?"*

*"But if you say: 'Write a 500-word essay about the American Revolution. Include 3 key events and explain why they were important. Use formal language and cite your sources.'"*

*"[Nod approvingly]"*

*"NOW they know exactly what to do!"*

*"That's exactly how prompts work with AI. Vague prompts = vague results. Clear prompts = amazing results!"*

---

### 💡 SIMPLE EXAMPLE (30 seconds)

**[SCRIPT]**

*"Here's a quick example:"*

*"Weak prompt: 'Write about dogs'"*

*"What does the AI even do? A story? Facts? Care guide?"*

*"Strong prompt: 'Write a 300-word beginner's guide to owning a golden retriever. Include sections on feeding, exercise, and grooming. Tone: friendly and helpful.'"*

*"See the difference?"*

*"The strong prompt tells the AI EXACTLY what to do. Length, topic, structure, tone - all specified!"*

---

### 🎯 HOOK FOR NEXT LESSON (15 seconds)

**[SCRIPT]**

*"And THAT is what a prompt is!"*

*"But here's the cool part - prompting is a SKILL. And like any skill, you can get better at it."*

*"In our next lesson, I'm going to break down the ANATOMY of a perfect prompt. You'll learn the exact elements that make prompts work."*

*"Trust me - this will change how you use AI forever!"*

*"See you in the next lesson!"*

## 🎯 Practice Prompt

Try writing a prompt to explain the concept of "gravity" to a 5-year-old.

**Weak:** "Explain gravity"

**Strong:** "Explain gravity in simple words that a 5-year-old can understand. Use at least one example from daily life. Keep it under 2 sentences."
